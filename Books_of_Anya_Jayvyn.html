Welcome to World of Anya Jayvyn !!

Anya's books revolve around angsty young adult and new adult romance.

You may expect a lot of steamy scenes too, and some suspense.

Writing is her therapy. She's just an ordinary person who likes to write to calm herself down.  

She go by Anya Jayvyn, a pen name.  Anya is her nickname. Jayvyn has the same meaning as her actual surname (which means Light).
